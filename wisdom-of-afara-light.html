Wisdom of Afara

Reclaiming our roots, restoring our wisdom,
reuniting our people across every continent.

We are an organisation dedicated to the education, empowerment and cultural healing of all people of African descent from the continent, from the Caribbean, from the Americas, from Europe and beyond. We believe the wisdom of our ancestors belongs to every descendant of Africa.

To Educate, Empower & Reconnect

About Florence & Afara

A Daughter of the Diaspora, Teaching for All

Florence Cook is a descendant of those Africans whose ancestors were taken from their homeland as enslaved people to build new worlds across the Americas and Caribbean. She carries their resilience, their pain, and their extraordinary wisdom within her.

Wisdom of Afara was born from her conviction that the teachings of African philosophy, culture, and ancestral heritage should belong equally to every person of African descent not just those living on the continent today.

"I am a descendant of those people so I care about them as much as Africans. This wisdom belongs to all of us."
— Florence Cook, Founder, Wisdom of Afara
