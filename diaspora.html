Souls of the South & North

African Americans

From the cotton fields of Mississippi to the brownstones of Harlem, from the migrations to Chicago to the protests in Selma a people who built a country and were never paid for it.

African Americans carry a particular gravity in the Diaspora. Most cannot trace a specific village. Most cannot speak the tongue their great-great-grandparents dreamed in. Yet from this very deprivation came one of the most influential cultures the modern world has ever produced...

Islands of the Diaspora

Afro-Caribbeans

Jamaica, Haiti, Trinidad, Barbados, Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Guyana a sea of small nations whose cultural reach spans the planet.

The Caribbean is where the Diaspora first improvised itself into existence. Stolen Africans of dozens of ethnicities were thrown together on plantations...
